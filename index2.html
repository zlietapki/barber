NYC Barbershop Museum

Historically men got their salons as Barbershops before women did. Men were working and they were treating themselves, getting at the same time haircut, manicure and shoe shine, as well as facial massage and adour Barbershop was a place of gathering, where men could spend time, socialize smoke a cigar and drink some liquor.​
